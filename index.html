Hi! I am an Assistant Professor of International Relations & Emerging Technologies at Carnegie Mellon University and the Stanton Nuclear Security Fellow at the Council on Foreign Relations. My research explores the impact of emerging technologies on international law and arms control. Previous affiliations include the Harvard Kennedy School, the Columbia-Harvard China and the World Program, and the US Fulbright Program to China. I received my PhD from Columbia University, along with a specialization in International Law from Columbia Law School.

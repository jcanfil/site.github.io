Recommendations

Recommendations are needed for virtually all jobs after college, and the best ones come from teachers who know you and the quality of your work well. I strongly suggest that students first seek out tenured professors to write their letters, since these carry the most weight. Quite understandably, however, not everyone has had the opportunity to develop close working relationships with tenured faculty before graduating.

If you took a class with me and need to ask for a recommendation letter, please email me with the following:
